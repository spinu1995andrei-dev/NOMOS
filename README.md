# NOMOS

**NOMOS** is an open public concept for a new digital infrastructure of value, trust, privacy, and free exchange.

It is not a company.  
It is not an investment product.  
It is not a promise of profit.  
It is not owned by one person, team, foundation, or government.

NOMOS is a starting point for an open protocol where value can exist without hidden emission, arbitrary censorship, forced intermediaries, or centralized control.

---

## Core Idea

> **A person’s value must belong to the person.  
> The rules of money must be open.  
> Individual privacy must be the norm.  
> No one should hold unilateral power over the system.**

---

## Main Principles

- Transparent and limited emission.
- Privacy by default.
- No central owner or administrator.
- No hidden premine or privileged addresses.
- No arbitrary freezing or confiscation.
- Full node accessibility for ordinary people.
- Open-source development.
- Free forking and independent implementations.
- Public rules, voluntary participation, and open criticism.
