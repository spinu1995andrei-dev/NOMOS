## What is NOMOS?

NOMOS is a **public manifesto and open protocol concept** — not a blockchain, not a token, not a startup.

It is a set of principles and architectural ideas for how a truly decentralized value protocol *could* work — one that belongs to no one, is controlled by no one, and exists for as long as the internet exists.

The name comes from the Greek **νόμος** — meaning *law of nature*. Not human law. Not corporate law. Mathematical law.

-----

## Why does this exist?

Bitcoin was a miracle. But it was built in 2008.

In 2026, we know things Satoshi didn’t:

- Quantum computers will break Bitcoin’s cryptography
- Mining corporations have centralized what was meant to be decentralized
- Every “decentralized” project still has a creator, a foundation, a team with power
- Privacy was added as an afterthought — not built into the core

**NOMOS asks one question:**

> *If Satoshi started today — what would he build differently?*

## The 7 Core Principles

These are not rules imposed by anyone. They are ideas open to debate, criticism, and improvement by anyone.

**1. Quantum-Resistant from Day One**
Built on post-quantum cryptography standards (NIST 2024). Not added later. Not patched. Native.

**2. Self-Updating Without Human Politics**
No votes. No forks. No “core developers” with power. An evolutionary algorithm that tests and adopts improvements automatically — like an immune system, not a parliament.

**3. Energy as Useful Work**
Mining energy goes toward real computation — scientific simulations, open AI models, medical research. The network earns from the world. Not from burning energy into nothing.

**4. Absolute Privacy at the Core**
Zero-knowledge proofs at the protocol level. You can prove you have value without revealing how much or where it came from. Not optional. Not a layer on top. The foundation.

**5. Anti-Capture by Design**
The more computational power you accumulate, the less proportional reward you receive. A laptop and a data center have fair, equal standing. Corporations cannot take over.

**6. No Creator Dependency**
The protocol is designed so that once launched, the creator becomes irrelevant. The code is the authority. The math is the governance.

**7. Self-Defense Against Centralization**
If any single entity approaches 40% network control — the consensus algorithm automatically shifts. The rules of the game change. The attacker’s power becomes worthless.
