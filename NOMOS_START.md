 # NOMOS Manifest: An Open Protocol for Honest, Private, Decentralized Value

## Introduction

NOMOS is a public, open, and forkable manifesto for building a new kind of digital value system — one where **no one controls the emission**, privacy is a default, rules are open, and everyone can participate equally.

NOMOS is not a company, a token, a startup, or an investment product. This is a blueprint, a vision, a contract for future finance.

---

## Principles

- **No hidden emission:** All issuance of value is transparent, capped, and auditable by anyone. No central bank, no "stealth inflation".
- **Privacy by default:** Balances and transfers are confidential and unlinkable, using advanced cryptography (e.g., zero-knowledge, ring signatures).
- **Open participation:** Anyone can join, build, use, or fork the protocol, with no central approval or admin powers.
- **Forkable and upgradeable:** The system can be improved or forked by open consensus processes. Upgrades are never hidden and never forced by an admin.
- **No foundation or privileged group:** There is no organization or team with special control or access.
- **Censorship resistance:** No one can arbitrarily freeze or block value. All transactions, so long as they follow transparent rules, are valid.
- **Fair start:** No premines, early investors, founders' tax, or invisible allocation of value. Every rule and supply algorithm is public from block zero.
- **Public good:** NOMOS is a public protocol for all humanity. Its main purpose is freedom and fairness, not profit or power for the few.

---

## The Problem

Most money, even in pseudo-cryptosystems, suffers from at least one of:
- Hidden/secret issuance (central banks, insiders, stealth-mint by admins, or whales)
- Lack of privacy by default (every transaction can be analyzed, tracked, and blocked)
- Control by privileged teams or organizations
- Unpredictable (or rigged) supply and monetary rules

NOMOS aims to eliminate all these risks by design — not by trust!

---

## Goals

1. **Design emission that is absolutely transparent and capped**
    - No human or hidden algorithm can “print” more
    - Rules are set once and for all, known and checkable by math
2. **Default privacy with open audit**
    - All balances and transactions are confidential, using cryptography, but anyone can verify the system’s correctness (no need to "trust" an admin)
    - Privacy is a right, not a feature for the lucky
3. **No central admin, no company, no “core team” with privileges**
    - No upgrades by decree, only by open consensus or forks
    - No critical dependence on any person, group, or jurisdiction
4. **Forkable forever**
    - Any group can propose or fork the code and rules. Protest by forking, not by arguing
5. **Completely open source and free license**
    - NOMOS is public domain (CC0/open source). Anyone can build, translate or implement — no restrictions.
6. **Fair launch and “genesis fairness”**
    - First block launches with clear/rigid supply and rules, no pre-allocation or secret deals

## Architecture (Draft)

- **Emission:** Algorithmic, capped, publicly auditable supply. No inflation after X years (parameters set in advance).
- **Consensus:** Decentralized, no trusted validators needed. (Can be based on PoW, PoS, or better new research — details for discussion).
- **Privacy:** All balances and transactions are private by default (zero-knowledge proofs, cryptonote/ring signatures, or next-gen cryptography).
- **Network:** Open peer-to-peer, global.
- **Upgrades/Forks:** Only by open proposal and social consensus, never by unilateral dev/admin decision.
