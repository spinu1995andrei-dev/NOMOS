# NOMOS Manifest

NOMOS is an open protocol for honest, private, and decentralized value exchange.

## Principles

- No one controls the system.
- Emission is transparent and limited.
- Privacy is a human right by default.
- Rules are public, open, and can be audited or forked.
- Anyone can participate.
- No admin, no founders' privileges.

## Goals

- Build a digital currency with:
  - Transparent and limited emission.
  - Privacy by default.
  - No central authority.
  - Fair start—no premine, no ICO, no hidden distribution.
  - Open for everyone to use and develop further.
  - Resistance to censorship.

## Status

This is currently a manifesto and draft technical vision.  
No product, no token, no hidden agenda.

Anyone can join, discuss, criticize, or create their own implementation.
